How it works

Three moves. Zero clutter.

This is intentionally simple: watch, capture, sync. The experience is designed to feel like a neon command console for learning.

01Keep YouTube open and start taking notes right where the content is happening.
02Add screenshots, drawings, and timestamps so every note has context.
03Use the app to carry those notes into your phone and continue later.

Why it feels different

It looks like a retro arcade cabinet, but it behaves like a study weapon.

The visual direction is bold on purpose. Slatify should feel tactile, memorable, and a little bit magical so the study process has personality.
